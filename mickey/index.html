Mickey convertor calculator

A mickey is a computer science term denoting the smallest percerviable distance a mouse moves. It is typically between 1/300th (0.003) and 1/200th (0.005)inches. 21 inches is between 4200 and 6300 mickeys, a mile is between 12.7 and 19.0 megamickeys, and the distance from San Francisco to New York is between 31.6 and 47.5 gigamickeys.
