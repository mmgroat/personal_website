Genealogy

The link below leads to the master index of my family tree, which contains about 13,000 relatives.  Web pages are created on demand with CGI scripting from a GedCom database, and include family pages along with pedigree and descendency charts. The site contains the ability to download a GedCom of ancestors or descendants of any individual. Modifications were made to the scripts to allow viewing of pedigree information of a large number of generations (over 150).
